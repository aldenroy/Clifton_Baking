Vanilla Buttercream

Ingredients:

1 cup butter soft(room temp)
3 cups powdered sugar
1 tbsp. vanilla
3-4 tsp. heavy cream

Instructions:

1. Whip butter with half of the powdered sugar
2. Mix in the rest of the powdered sugar, cream, and vanilla
3. Whip well until you have stiff peaks
