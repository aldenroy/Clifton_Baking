Chocolate Almond Espresso Cupcakes

Ingredients:

Dry:

2 and 1/2 cups flour
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. baking soda
1 and 1/2 tsp. baking powder
small handfull of crushed almonds

Wet:

1/2 cup butter softened
1 cup white sugar
1 tsp. almond extract
1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
2 eggs
1/4 cup sour cream

Instructions:

1. Mix dry ingredients together with whisk. Make sure almonds are small
2. Cream butter and sugar, then add the rest of the wet ingredients and mix well
3. Slowly add dry mix to wet mix while stirring and mix well
4. Pour into tins
5. Bake at 350 degrees fahrenheit for 12 minutes or until toothpickcomes out clean
