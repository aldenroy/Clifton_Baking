Zucchini Pizza

Ingredients:

Zucchini
Tomato sauce
Mozzarella
Pepperoni
Pizza dough or pre-made crust

Instructions:

1. Broil zucchini for 4 minutes
2. Top dough with sauce
3. Add mozzarella to taste
4. Add pepperoni and broiled zucchini
5. Bake/broil for 3 minutes or until cheese is melted
