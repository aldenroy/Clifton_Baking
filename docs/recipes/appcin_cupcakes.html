Apple Cinnamon Cupcakes

Ingredients:

Dry:

1 and 1/2 cups flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. baking powder
1 tbsp. cinnamon

Wet:

3/4 cup white sugar
2 eggs
1/4 cup sour cream
1/2 cup hot water
1 diced apple
1 and 1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1 tsp. vanilla

Instructions:

1. Mix dry ingredients together well
2. Mix wet ingredients together well
3. Slowly add wet mix to dry mix while stirring and mix well
4. Pour into tins
5. Bake at 350 degrees fahrenheit for 12-15 minutes or until toothpickcomes out clean
