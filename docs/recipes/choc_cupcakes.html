Chocolate Cupcakes

Ingredients:

Dry:

2 and 1/2 cups flour
1 and 1/2 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. baking soda
pinch of salt
1/2 cup chocolate

Wet:

1/2 cup butter
1 cup sugar
2 eggs
1/2 cup hot water
1/2 cup sour cream
splash of vegetable oil
handful of chocolate chips, melted
1/2 tsp. vanilla

Instructions:

1. Mix dry ingredients
2. Mix wet ingredients in own bowl
3. pour wet ingredients into dry and mix
4. Pour into tins
5. Bake at 350 degrees fahrenheit for 12 minutes or until toothpickcomes out clean
