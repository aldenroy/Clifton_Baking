Raspberry Vanilla Swirl Buttercream

Ingredients:

3-4 cups powdered sugar
1/2 cup butter
1/2 tsp. vanilla
3-4 tsp. whipping cream

Instructions:

1. Mix ingredients
2. Separate buttercream into 2 bowls
3. Put 1/2 tsp. vanilla into vanilla frosting, put 1 tsp. raspbery extract into
the raspbery bowl, along with purple food coloring
4. Use toothpick to swirl the two frostings together
