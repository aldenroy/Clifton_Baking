Chocolate Berry Blast Cupcakes

Ingredients:

For chocolate base:
Dry:

1 cup flour
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 cup cocoa powder
1/2 tsp. baking soda

Wet:

1/4 tsp. softened butter
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup brown sugar
1oz melted chocolate chips
1 tsp. vanilla
1 egg(optional)
1/4 cup sour cream

For berry top:
Dry:

1 cup flour
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 cup blueberry powder
1/2 tsp. baking soda

Wet:

1/4 cup softened butter
1/2 cup white sugar
1 egg(optional)
2/3 cup hot water
1/4 cup sour cream

Instructions:

Chocolate batter instructions:

1. Mix dry ingredients in medium bowl
2. Beat butter and sugar in large bowl
3. Add rest of the wet ingredients
4. Slowly add dry ingredients
5. Mix well and pour into tins

Berry top instructions:

1. Mix dry ingredients well
2. Beat butter and sugar in large bowl
3. Add rest of the wet ingredients
4. Add dry to wet
5. Mix well, pour on top of chocolate mixture

Final steps:

Bake cupcakes at 360 degrees fahrenheit for 16 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean
