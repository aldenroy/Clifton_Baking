Chocolate Overnight Oats

Ingredients:

1/2 cup rolled oats
1/3 milk of choice
1/4 cup greek yogurt
1 tsp. maple syrup
1 tsp. cocoa powder
Diced strawberries to taste

Instructions:

1. Mix ingredients together well, except for the strawberries
2. Put in jar
3. Top with strawberries
4. Refrigerate overnight
